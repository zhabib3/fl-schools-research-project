If you would like to contact a member of our staff, please feel free to email them using a link provided below. If your question requires speaking to a teacher, please make your call before or after classroom hours or you may leave a message with our front office staff.

To provide your child with the most in-class assistance, we will not disrupt the classroom unless it is an emergency situation.

Thank you for your cooperation.

Mission Statement
As a national family of public charter school campuses, Imagine Schools partners with parents and guardians in the education of their children by providing high quality schools that prepare students for lives of leadership, accomplishment, and exemplary character.

Vision Statement
Developing Character, Enriching Minds: Imagine Schools’ vision is for every student to reach his or her full potential and discover the pathways for life-long success.
