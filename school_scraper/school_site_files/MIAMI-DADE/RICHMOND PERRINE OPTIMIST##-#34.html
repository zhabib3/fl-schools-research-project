Welcome to Outreach!

We cannot change the wind, but we can adjust the sails. The mission of the Educational Alternative Outreach Program (EAOP) is the development of strategies tailored to the needs of children at risk. We take pride in the multiple accomplishments of our student population.

The Educational Alternative Outreach Progam is an educational program for the at-risk student population. In essence, we strive for perfection, knowing that the ongoing process will be ever changing. Our overall goal is to provide challenging students with opportunities that will hopefully change their chartered courses in life. In essence, the overall goal and objective of the Education Alternative Outreach Program are that “We are striving to eradicate the school to jailhouse pipeline!”
