Overview

  • Broward County Public Schools is proud to be our community’s largest employer, with more than 34,000 full- and part-time employees.

    The District has been commended for excellence in compensation, professional support, effective management and operations, and career and leadership opportunities. Honored as one of the first eight Great Districts for Great Teachers in 2017, BCPS is recognized as a national leader for exceptional programs and policies on recruiting, encouraging, supporting and retaining great teachers

     

     For all staff resources and services, please log in to Insite.
