Overview

  •

  • Employee & Labor Relations is committed to providing the highest standard of excellence and cooperation between employees, labor, and management with the goal of ultimately providing all students with the most productive learning environment.

    There are seven (7) collective bargaining agreements and three (3) meet & confer groups that include: 

    Collective Bargaining Agreements

    Broward Teachers Union (BTU):

    • Teachers (BTU-EP)
    • Education Support Professionals (BTU-ESP)
    • Technical Support Professionals (BTU-TSP)

    Federation of Public Employees (FOPE):

    • Facilities, Maintenance, Security Specialists/Campus Monitors and Transportation
    • Secretarial/Clerical
    • Food Service

    Broward County Police Benevolent Association, Inc. (PBA)

    • Detectives

    Meet and Confer Groups

    Educational Support & Management Association of Broward, Inc. (ESMAB)

    Broward Principals & Assistants Association (BPAA)

    Confidential Office Personnel Association (COPA)
